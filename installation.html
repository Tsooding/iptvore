Installation Guide

Step-by-step instructions to get iptvore running on your device

Amazon Firestick Installation

1

Open the Appstore

From your Firestick home screen, navigate to the Appstore and search for "iptvore"

2

Download & Install

Select the iptvore app and click "Download" then "Install". Wait for the installation to complete.

3

Launch the App

Once installed, click "Open" to launch iptvore for the first time.

4

Login to Your Account

Enter your iptvore credentials (email and password) to access your subscription.

5

Start Streaming

Browse channels, select your content, and start enjoying iptvore on your Firestick.

💡 Tips:

  • Ensure your Firestick is connected to a stable internet connection
  • Keep the app updated for the best performance
  • Use the remote to navigate easily through the interface

Smart TV Installation (LG/Samsung)

1

Access Your TV's App Store

On your LG or Samsung Smart TV, open the built-in app store (LG: LG Content Store, Samsung: Samsung Apps)

2

Search for iptvore

Use the search function to find "iptvore" in the app store.

3

Install the Application

Click "Install" and wait for the app to download and install on your TV.

4

Open iptvore

Launch the app from your TV's home screen or app menu.

5

Sign In

Enter your iptvore login credentials to access your account.

💡 Tips:

  • Make sure your TV is connected to WiFi or Ethernet
  • Update your TV's firmware for compatibility
  • Use your TV remote or a compatible Bluetooth remote for navigation

Mobile Installation (Android & iOS)

Android Installation

1

Open Google Play Store

Launch the Google Play Store app on your Android device.

2

Search for iptvore

Tap the search icon and type "iptvore".

3

Install

Tap "Install" and allow the necessary permissions.

4

Open the App

Once installed, tap "Open" to launch iptvore.

5

Login

Enter your email and password to access your account.

iOS Installation

1

Open App Store

Launch the App Store on your iPhone or iPad.

2

Search iptvore

Tap the search tab and search for "iptvore".

3

Download

Tap the cloud icon to download the app.

4

Install

Authenticate with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password.

5

Launch & Login

Open the app and sign in with your iptvore credentials.

💡 Tips:

  • Ensure you have sufficient storage space on your device
  • Use a stable WiFi connection for faster downloads
  • Keep your app updated for new features and bug fixes

Laptop & PC Installation

1

Visit iptvore Website

Go to www.streamvault.com and navigate to the Downloads section.

2

Download the Application

Select your operating system (Windows or macOS) and click "Download".

3

Run the Installer

Locate the downloaded file and double-click to run the installer.

4

Follow Installation Wizard

Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation process.

5

Launch & Sign In

Open iptvore from your desktop or applications menu and log in.

💡 Tips:

  • Disable antivirus temporarily if installation fails
  • Ensure you have administrator privileges
  • Use a web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari) as an alternative

Apple TV Installation

1

Open the App Store

From your Apple TV home screen, navigate to the App Store.

2

Search for iptvore

3

Download & Install

Open the App

MAG Box Installation

1

Access Settings

On your MAG Box remote, press the "Menu" button to access the main menu.

2

Navigate to Applications

Go to "Applications" or "Installed Apps" section.

3

Add Application

Select "Add Application" and enter the iptvore application URL or code.

4

Install

Confirm the installation and wait for the application to be added to your device.

5

Launch & Configure

Open iptvore and enter your login credentials to start streaming.

💡 Tips:

  • Ensure your MAG Box has sufficient storage space
  • Keep your device firmware updated
  • Contact support if you need the application URL

Enigma2 Installation

1

Access the Plugin Manager

On your Enigma2 device, go to Menu > Plugins > Plugin Browser.

2

Search for iptvore

Search for "iptvore" in the available plugins list.

3

Install Plugin

Select iptvore and click "Install" to download and install the plugin.

4

Restart Your Device

Restart your Enigma2 device to activate the plugin.

5

Configure & Login

Open the iptvore plugin and enter your account credentials.

💡 Tips:

  • Ensure your Enigma2 device has internet connectivity
  • Update your device's image to the latest version
  • Some plugins may require additional dependencies
