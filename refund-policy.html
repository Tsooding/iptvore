Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Free Trial Period

iptvore offers a 7-day free trial for new users. During this trial period, you have full access to all features of your selected plan at no cost. Your credit card will not be charged during the trial period.

2. Trial Cancellation

You can cancel your free trial at any time before the trial period ends. If you cancel before the trial expires, you will not be charged. Cancellation can be done through your account settings or by contacting our support team.

3. Paid Subscription Refunds

After your free trial ends and you begin a paid subscription, the following refund policy applies:

  • 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee: If you are not satisfied with iptvore within 30 days of your first paid subscription, you are eligible for a full refund of your subscription fee.
  • Refund Request Process: To request a refund, contact our support team at refunds@streamvault.com with your account information and reason for the refund request.
  • Processing Time: Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days after approval.

4. Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a refund if:

  • You request the refund within 30 days of your first paid subscription charge
  • Your account is in good standing (no violations of Terms of Service)
  • You have not already received a refund for the same billing period
  • You are requesting a refund for a legitimate reason (technical issues, service dissatisfaction, etc.)

5. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • Refund requests made more than 30 days after the initial charge
  • Partial month refunds (refunds are only for full billing cycles)
  • Refunds for accounts suspended due to Terms of Service violations
  • Refunds for users who have already received a refund in the current billing year
  • Refunds for technical issues caused by the user's device or internet connection

6. Subscription Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle. You will retain access to iptvore until the end of your billing period, and no refund will be issued for the remaining time in your current billing cycle.

7. Billing Issues

If you notice any billing errors or unauthorized charges, please contact us immediately at billing@streamvault.com. We will investigate and resolve billing disputes within 10 business days.

8. Payment Method Changes

You can update your payment method at any time through your account settings. If a payment fails, we will attempt to charge your account again. If payment continues to fail, your account may be suspended.

9. Promotional Offers

Refunds for promotional subscription offers (discounted rates, special bundles, etc.) are subject to the terms of the specific promotion. Please review the promotion details before subscribing.

10. Refund Method

Approved refunds will be credited back to the original payment method used for the subscription. If the original payment method is no longer available, we will work with you to arrange an alternative refund method.

11. Contact Us for Refund Requests

To request a refund or discuss billing concerns, please contact our support team:

12. Policy Changes

iptvore reserves the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to the website. We will notify users of significant changes via email.

13. Dispute Resolution

If you have a dispute regarding a refund decision, you may appeal to our management team. Please provide detailed information about your case, and we will review it within 15 business days.

14. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback through your credit card company instead of requesting a refund through our official process, your account may be permanently suspended, and you may lose access to iptvore services.
